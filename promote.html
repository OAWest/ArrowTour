  • A Note to you:

    Lodge and Council Leadership,



    I would like to say thank you for your hard work and dedication to the BSA. It is volunteers that run this program, and your help ensures the success of the Order of the Arrow and BSA. To ensure that programs run well, I need your help. Enclosed in this packet is marketing and promotional materials for ArrowTour. While the tour can show up, it will not be any fun without having people show up. So please use this material to share when and where ArrowTour will be when it is in your area, and encourage people to come. This will be a great opportunity to reach out to members and non-members alike, and show what the BSA and Order of the Arrow is all about.

    Included Items:
    • • Brochure template for printing and distribution
    • • Flyers with fillable information about time and place of ArrowTour in your area
    • • Website Banners and link button to be place on council/lodge websites
    • • Template Facebook and Twitter posts, along with information regarding the ArrowTour West Facebook page and events
    • • Promotional Videos

    Things you can do specifically to help promote ArrowTour (available at http://www.western.oa-bsa.org/atwestern/promote.html)
    • • Re-post content from the Western Region facebook page onto your lodge/section and personal walls - Facebook.com/OAWest)
    • • Print and post the posters provided at lodge, section, council, and any other appropriate venue
    • • Place the ArrowTour banners and buttons on lodge and council websites with links to Western.OA-BSA.org/ATWestern or Bit.ly/ATWestern
    • • Verbally promote ArrowTour through word-of-mouth

    Thank you for your hard work and time in bringing ArrowTour to your councils and lodges. It is volunteers like you that make this program possible. Feel free to contact me (by email preferably) with any comments, questions, or concerns you may have.

    WWW,
    Michael Goswick
    PR/Marketing Lead
    ArrowTour Road Crew
    atweststaff@western.oa-bsa.org
